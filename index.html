Project Three

Explore

For this five day group project, we built a full-stack application to plan and display a round-the-world trip. Users could browse the famous sites that were included on our database and add them to their dashboard. They could then visualise their trip on their dashboard's map and change the order of their journey using a react drag-and-drop feature.

Software:

React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bulma, MongoDB, Mongoose, Express, Axios, Mocha, Chai, Webpack.

Project Four

Sunset Barlevard

In my final project at General Assembly, two classmates and I built a full stack application where users could create and display their own bar crawls. Software:

React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bulma, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, Flask, Sinon, Enzyme, Mocha, Chai, Webpack.
